About

It can be both thrilling and frustrating to plan a trip on our own since it requires us to collect information from various sources back and forth before we can finalize everything into a file. What if we can spend less time generating our own travel plans in one step? With this website, users can customize their travel plans based on their preferences. By answering simple questions, a well-developed one-day travel plan will be made and users can save it as a picture so whenever they want to set off, they will be ready to go immediately.
